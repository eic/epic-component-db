### Component Database (CDB) — Assembly/Design

---

#### Creating a design

To create a "design", i.e. an assembly of inventory items, one first creates placeholders (references to the _catalog_), which
defines the basic structure of a design. Then, each placeholder is filled with a reference to the appropriate _inventory_ item,
i.e. a physical unit that corresponds to the catalog but may have its own unique set of properties.
